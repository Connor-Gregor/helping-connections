Building Positive Connections

Our Mission

Helping Connections is dedicated to bridging the gap between volunteers and individuals experiencing homelessness in the Milwaukee area. Our platform provides a simple and accessible way for people to request help and for volunteers to respond to those needs quickly.

How the Platform Works

Individuals can submit requests for support such as food, clothing, transportation, or other essential resources. Volunteers can browse these requests, claim them, and provide assistance directly through the platform.

Community Impact

By connecting compassionate volunteers with those who need support, Helping Connections aims to strengthen communities and create meaningful opportunities for people to help one another.
